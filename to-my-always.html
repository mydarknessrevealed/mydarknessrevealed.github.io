To My Always

“To My Always”
In the whispers of the wind,
I hear your name—
not spoken, but sung,
a melody etched in the threads of my soul.
The river remembers us,
its waters carrying the echoes
of laughter and tears,
of stolen glances and promises
we never stopped meaning.
Time tried to scatter us,
to erase the lines we drew in the sand,
but love—
our love—
is the tide that returns,
smoothing every scar.
I saw you first in a mayfly storm,
your eyes the only light I’ve ever needed.
Do you remember, Sabrina?
When the world stood still,
and the air between us danced
with the weight of eternity?
Every moment since has been chasing that—
that feeling, that truth.
And when I found you again,
it wasn’t chance.
It was gravity pulling home
what was always meant to be whole.
You are the stars in my night,
the fire in my winter,
the dream that never fades.
And though the years have tried to bury us,
I still stand, digging, unearthing,
finding you in every corner of my heart.

- David A. Mishoe
