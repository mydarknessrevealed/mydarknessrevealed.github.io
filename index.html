Welcome to the void!

Code. Chaos. Carnage.

This is not a place for the faint-hearted.

This is where entropy reigns,

where poetry bleeds, and where the

digital and the damned collide.

I build, I break, I resurrect.

Code is my weapon, words are my invocation,

and the abyss is always watching.

Step inside. Explore the wreckage.

See what lurks beneath the surface.

You have been warned.
