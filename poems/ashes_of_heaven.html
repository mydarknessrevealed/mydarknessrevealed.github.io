Ashes of Heaven

I tread the flames with stories to tell,
Sins of Heaven, Angel’s skin like bread, unlevin’—pleasures of Hell.
The ringing of the bell,
The hissing crackle of fire, honey-sweet smoke upon the pyre.

Salvation of Hell, temptation of Heaven,
Once a heart bound so true,
Yet the reverse view is
With each kiss, I carve your scars through and through,
The beast it laughs, and I laugh too.

David A. Mishoe
