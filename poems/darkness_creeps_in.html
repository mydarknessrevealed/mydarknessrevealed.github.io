Darkness Creeps In

Darkness creeps in,
I shiver, then I move again.
My head is heavy,
As I recall the sin.

There comes to one and all an end,
Be it joyous or regretful,
It’s where we’ve been.

The dutiful watcher,
Counts us all,
Listening for the beck and call.
A tune played for us all.

Be ye humble or stand tall,
In the end, you're still
Insignificant and small.

David A. Mishoe
