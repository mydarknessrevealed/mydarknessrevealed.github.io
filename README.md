## Hi there 👋 I'm David and this is 

My Darkness Revealed - Poetry Collection

Welcome to the official repository for **My Darkness Revealed**, a collection of poetry.

Here, I share my poetic explorations of memory, identity, and the darker aspects of life. Each piece is a reflection on the complexities of existence, written in a lyrical and sometimes haunting style.

## What to Expect

- **Poetry**: Works that loop and return to their starting points, often exploring personal struggles, memory, and the chaos of life.
- **Themes**: Rebellion, introspection, loss, and transformation.
